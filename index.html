The Sushi Tei
Experience

At The Sushi Tei, we bring the art of Japanese cuisine to life—
one handcrafted roll at a time. From the freshest sashimi to
our signature sushi creations, every bite is a celebration of
flavor, tradition, and craftsmanship.

From Japan to,
Bangladesh

Bringing authentic Japanese flavors straight
to your table, crafted with the freshest ingredients and timeless traditions.

Menu

California Roll

A refreshing mix of crab stick, avocado, and cucumber, rolled inside-out with a coating of sesame seeds—perfect for sushi beginners and experts alike.

Dragon Roll

An eye-catching roll featuring eel, cucumber, and avocado, drizzled with sweet unagi sauce and topped with sesame seeds for a rich, smoky taste.

Spicy Tuna Roll

A fiery twist on the classic tuna roll, made with tender tuna mixed in spicy mayo, wrapped in seaweed and rice for a flavorful kick.

Salmon Nigiri

A timeless favorite fresh, buttery slices of salmon draped over perfectly seasoned sushi rice, delivering a melt-in-your-mouth experience.
